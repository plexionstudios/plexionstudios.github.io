About Me

The Beginnings

My first video back in 2018 was this one, I simply taught people how to do something in Scratch.

If i'm being honest, the only things bad about this video are the lack of audio and the text that moves at

20 miles per hour. This eventually became my most viewed video, somehow?

Shortly After..

Seeing that people liked the video I thought I would make another video. The first idea that came to my

head was, "how about i teach people how to draw a sword!". I still question what this is actually teaching people

to this day. I ended up editing the video and re-releasing it for Plexmas.

200 VIEWS

In April 2019, I published a video called 'PewDiePie Congratulations - 8 Bit Mix' featuring a mix of an 8-bit

congratulations remix and the official vocals. The video became my 2nd most viewed video hitting 288 views total!

It stayed like this for quite a while.

Plexmas 2019

Walking to school one day I came up with this idea, "How about I make a special series for Christmas this year, it won't

be that bad". I then created it and ran it from the 13th of December to, what was going to be the 25th but was eventually

cancelled on the 17th. That doesn't mean it won't happen this year. Ehehe~ ;)

The Key to Success

After school on a Friday, I recorded a video on a new game made by a YouTuber I watch, DaniDev! I downloaded the game and

played it while recording. I edited it and uploaded it, not expecting much attention on the video. The day after, I wake up

to have a notification feed flooded with subscriber and comment notifications. It was surreal to see and I thank you all so much!

Present Day

I appreciate everyone who watches my videos and subscribes, it means so much! Now I create a range of videos on

different games and topics. If you have any suggestions or feedback you can email me at plexionnet@gmail.com and talk

to me; i may not respond instantly.
