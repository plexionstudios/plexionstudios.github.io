WELCOME TO SKYPLEX!

Skyplex is a different take on the vanilla Minecraft experience allowing you to earn money and purchase items.

With these items, you can fight off mobs and become as rich as you can.

GETTING STARTED

When you're starting out on Skyplex you can choose one of 3 professions: miner, fisherman or a lumberjack.
