How it begun

My channel has come a long way since 2018 and there were some very crucial moments that led up to where I am today.

Scratch Tutorial - Simple Top Down Scrolling

October 2018

Where it all started.

Surprisingly enough, I didn't start my channel solely based on Minecraft. My first video I ever uploaded to YouTube was a tutorial for Scratch. This became my most viewed video for way too long.


Image source: Scratch Tutorial - Simple Top Down Scrolling

How to make a 256x Diamond Sword

October 2018

And it continued..

After that incredible Scratch video I decided to switch up the content (after only one upload). For some reason the I "taught" people how to draw a 256x Diamond Sword. To this day, scientists can still not uncover what ancient techniques are being taught here.


Image source: How to make a 256x Diamond Sword

PewDiePie Congratulations: 8-Bit Mix

April 2019

200 VIEWS!

In April 2019, I published a video called 'PewDiePie Congratulations - 8 Bit Mix' featuring a mix of an 8-bit congratulations remix and the official vocals. At one point, the video reached the #2 spot on my channel's most viewed shelf until being overtaken later on.


Image source: PewDiePie Congratulations: 8-Bit Mix

Plexmas #1: Using SNOWBALLS as a Weapon in Skywars?

December 2019

Plexmas 2019.

Walking to school one day I came up with this idea, "How about I make a special series for Christmas this year, it won't be that bad". I then created it and ran it from the 13th of December to, what was going to be the 25th but was eventually cancelled on the 17th. I really didn't think that through.


Image source: Plexmas #1: Using SNOWBALLS as a Weapon in Skywars?

Playing KARLSON by Dani! (karlson 3d)

January 2020

KARLSON

After school on a Friday, I recorded a video on a new game made by a YouTuber I watch, DaniDev! I downloaded the game and played it while recording. I edited it and uploaded it, not expecting much attention on the video. The day after, I wake up to have a notification feed flooded with subscriber and comment notifications. It was surreal to see and I thank you all so much!


Image source: Playing KARLSON by Dani! (karlson 3d)

THE 100 SUB SPECIAL

April 2020

The Grand Milestone

On the 11th of April 2020, I reached the milestone of 100 SUBS! This then lead to a grand 100 sub special video featuring a compilation of my favourite videos and you boys!


Image source: THE 100 SUB SPECIAL

The Minecraft MANHUNT.

August 2020

Shift in Content Quality

In August, I made a big change to the quality of my content & thumbnails that still continues to be worked on to this day. It was at this point that the thumbnails began to be more refined and the videos became more unique and structured.


Image source: The Minecraft MANHUNT.

The #PLEXION2020 Rewind.

November 2020

AGAIN?!

After the already incredible milestone earlier this year, on the 19th of November 2020 I reached 200 SUBSCRIBERS. WHAT.


Image source: The #PLEXION2020 Rewind.

Minecraft but the VOID RISES

January 2021

100 in a day

To start of #PLEXION2021 with a bang, the saga of Minecraft Tournament videos started. The first one being Void Rising. It took just over a day for the video to reach 100 views. WHAT. You boys are genuinely insane and I can't thank you enough.


Image source: Minecraft but the VOID RISES

Present Day

The Aftermath
