TIMELINE

A short but sweet history of my channel.

Where it all started.

My first video back in 2018 was this one, I simply taught people how to do something in Scratch. If i'm being honest, the only things bad about this video are the lack of audio and the text that moves at 2000 miles per hour. This eventually became my most viewed video, somehow?

And it continued..

Seeing that people liked the video I thought I would make another video. The first idea that came to my head was, "how about i teach people how to draw a sword!". I still question what this is actually teaching people to this day. I ended up editing the video and re-releasing it for Plexmas.

200 VIEWS!

In April 2019, I published a video called 'PewDiePie Congratulations - 8 Bit Mix' featuring a mix of an 8-bit congratulations remix and the official vocals. The video became my 2nd most viewed video hitting 288 views total! It stayed like this for quite a while.

Plexmas 2019.

Walking to school one day I came up with this idea, "How about I make a special series for Christmas this year, it won't be that bad". I then created it and ran it from the 13th of December to, what was going to be the 25th but was eventually cancelled on the 17th. That doesn't mean it won't happen this year.

The Key to Success

After school on a Friday, I recorded a video on a new game made by a YouTuber I watch, DaniDev! I downloaded the game and played it while recording. I edited it and uploaded it, not expecting much attention on the video. The day after, I wake up to have a notification feed flooded with subscriber and comment notifications. It was surreal to see and I thank you all so much!

The Grand Milestone

On the 11th of April, I reached the milestone of 100 SUBS! This then lead to a grand 100 sub special video featuring a compilation of my favourite videos and you boys!

The Aftermath

I'm quite happy with the content I'm pushing out to my channel these days. A lot more time and effort is put into each video so I would appreciate it if you could subscribe!
